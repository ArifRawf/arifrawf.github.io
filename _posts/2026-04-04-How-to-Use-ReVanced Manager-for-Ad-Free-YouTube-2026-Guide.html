Our main goal is to enjoy an ad-free YouTube experience, and ReVanced Manager is the key tool to achieve it. ReVanced Manager (RV Manager) is an all-in-one solution that helps users easily download, install, and manage ReVanced apps and modded APKs in a safe, reliable, and user-friendly way.

Follow this step-by-step guide to get started with ReVanced Manager and enjoy an ad-free YouTube experience in 2026

Step 1: Download Required Files

First, go to vanced.to. There, you will see 3 download options:

  • ReVanced Manager
  • ReVanced YouTube
  • MicroG

Download all of them to continue. You can also use ReVanced Manager to download everything automatically.

Step 2: Why Do You Need MicroG?

Before installing YouTube ReVanced, you need to understand MicroG. MicroG is an open-source alternative to Google Play Services. It allows apps like YouTube ReVanced to run properly on your device without official Google services.

Why MicroG is important:

  • Helps you sign in to your Google account
  • Ensures YouTube features work correctly
  • Required for non-root users

Step 3: Install ReVanced Manager

  • Install the ReVanced Manager APK
  • Open the app
  • Allow required permissions

This app will help you manage and install ReVanced apps easily.

Step 4: Install YouTube ReVanced

  • Open ReVanced Manager
  • Select YouTube
  • Apply patches (recommended settings)
  • Install the app

Step 5: Install and Set Up MicroG

  • Install MicroG APK
  • Open it and sign in with your Google account
  • Your YouTube ReVanced will now work perfectly

Important: Keep MicroG running in the background. Open MicroG first, then minimize it before opening YouTube.

Final Words

If your goal is to watch YouTube without ads, ReVanced Manager is the perfect solution. With easy setup and powerful features, you can enjoy a smooth and premium-like experience for free.
